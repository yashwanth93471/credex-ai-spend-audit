## How it works (3 steps)

1. **List your AI tools** — what you pay for, how many seats, what your team uses it for.
2. **Get a personalised report** — plan downgrades, seat cuts, cheaper alternatives, credit programs you're missing.
3. **Save it, share it, send it to finance** — your link works without a login.

## Sample finding card (social proof)

> **Downgrade ChatGPT Team → Plus × 4 seats**
> You have 4 ChatGPT Team seats at $30/mo each = $120/mo. With your stated
> use case (general writing, no shared workspace), 4 individual Plus seats
> at $20/mo each cover the same workflow.
> **Save: $40/mo · $480/yr**

## Trust signals (what to add later)

- Number of audits run
- Aggregate savings surfaced ("$X found across N audits")
- Sample report screenshots (with permission)

## What we deliberately don't say

- "AI-powered audit" — the catalog and rules are deterministic; the AI part
  is just the personalised narrative.
- "Save thousands" — claims must be specific, sourced, and conservative.
- Anything about "enterprise grade" or "secure" — we're not selling that yet.

## Tone

Direct, slightly punchy, not corporate. Numbers over adjectives.
Second person. Active voice.
