# User interviews

> Day 1 placeholder — interviews are conducted across Days 2–4 and summarised
> here. The intent is 3–5 short conversations with people in the ICP, focused
> on validating the audit findings (not the UI).

## Interview goals

1. **Are the findings believable?** — show a sample audit, ask which
   findings they'd act on and which they'd dismiss.
2. **What would they pay for the next step?** — past the report, what does
   the user actually need from Credex?
3. **What's missing?** — which tool / plan / seat dimension did we not ask
   about that would change their answer?

## Interview script

1. *"Walk me through your team's AI tool stack — what's on a credit card statement right now?"*
2. *(Show them a redacted sample report.)* *"Tell me what you're seeing on this page."*
3. *"Which finding would you act on this week? Which would you ignore?"*
4. *"If a tool like this existed, what would have to be true for you to forward it to your CFO?"*
5. *"What's a question we should have asked you in the form, but didn't?"*
